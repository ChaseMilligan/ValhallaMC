ValhallaMC SMP is an economy & adventure focused survival server with a player-driven economy and a heavier focus on territory control in v2.1. Enchants and effects have been toned down, villagers are back to vanilla (ish), the shop and economy are upgraded, worldgen/structures are improved, and the resource packs are merged for smoother updates and custom items, textures, and 40+ custom music discs.

The biggest change is Lands: a full claim system with upkeep, taxes, nations, and war. You can protect your builds by claiming chunks, but your Land must stay funded — upkeep is removed daily, and if you go broke your claims become unprotected and fair game for anyone to steal, conquer, or flatten.

Build your empire by grinding for Fehu (ᚠ), exploring for rare loot, investing in claims, and teaming up with allies. Lands can form Nations, declare war, and conquer territory even when you’re offline — so don’t get comfortable. The end goal is simple:

Get rich. Hold land. Become the #1 player on the server.

Can't Move?

If you can't move upon connecting to the server, just wait a minute or two since you are likely downloading the multiple custom resource packs used for the custom items and music discs. This should not take as long after your first time connecting. If you are still having trouble, make sure your game is on the correct version (1.21.10).

Can't Build?

You have to link your account in discord first to get permissions to play. You can leave the Discord server once your account is linked if you want.

Basic Gameplay Loop

  1. Join the server
  2. Link your Minecraft + Discord accounts (required to start playing)
  3. Type /wild to teleport to a random spot in the wilderness
  4. Place a Camp for temporary land protection
  5. Earn your first ᚠ1,000
  6. Build a steady income source by selling:
    • Items (/askaup)
    • Experience Points (/sellxp)
  7. Use /lands to create a Land and start claiming territory
  8. Claim your first chunk using:
    9. /lands claim radius 0 (claims 1 chunk)
  9. Keep your Land funded (/lands deposit [amount])
  10. 1 claimed chunk costs ᚠ1,010 every 24 hours
  11. Explore and loot to find rare items and earn more ᚠ
  12. Make allies and trust players in your Land so they can:
    • Spend ᚠ500 to teleport to your Land’s spawn
    • Use extra ᚠ to:
      • Claim more chunks
      • Trade with players
      • Buy from the server store
  13. Keep earning ᚠ so your claims don’t expire
  14. Form Nations with other Land owners, rent subareas of your lands to tenants, and wage war against your foes!
  15. The ultimate goal is to be the richest player on the server.

What's in v2

  • Switching from UberEnchants → CustomAnvil + ExcellentEnchants. Enchantments are still very powerful comapred to vanilla (level max has been doubled), but not to the same degree, and getting them is done in a more vanilla feeling way. Instead of using commands for the highest level enchants, the level cap of the anvil has been lifted so it can be used to combine enchanted books, and identical items regardless of their current level requirement.
  • Villager trades reverted to vanilla.
  • New shop with a bigger item list and price tags.
  • Same player shops and auction house.
  • New economy plugin for better scalability.
  • Player Orders allow you to make orders for items that other players can fulfill to get paid.
  • Keeping Stellarity End improvements; removing Beyond the Clouds.
  • Merged resourcepacks so we can have all of our favourite reskins, and I have added 40+ Custom Music Discs that can be collected from creepers, and found in naturally generated chests + fragments.
  • Overworld generation datapacks for more interesting terrain and a variety of new structures.
  • Yggdrasil is a datapack that adds a quest line, and massive structures that can take hours to fully explore packed with rare loot. It also introduces a new dimension full of labyrinthine dungeons.
  • PlayerStats can be shared in chat with leaderboards for every stat, and Tool Stats keeps track of things like when your tools and armour were crafted/looted, how much damage they have dealt/taken, or how many blocks they have broken as NBT tags on the items themselves.
  • Withdraw Fehu (ᚠ) into bank notes; sell EXP/levels for Fehu.
  • Tool Trims are a great addition I can't believe aren't in the base game. There are now 4 lootable tool trim templates that can be used on all tools/weapons with over 1200 combinations.
  • Smart Spawners can be collected with silk touch, and offer easy item and xp collection from spawners collected naturally, or purchased.
  • Food Balance gives all the different foods in the game unique effects, and tries to make them more balanced in power.
  • Coinflipz is a fun mini-game that allows players to wager Fehu on a simple coin flip or roulette.
  • Mooshroom Energy & MooshDonalds is a datapack that transforms underused Minecraft items into powerful consumables.
  • Horde Nights occur once every 35 in game nights for some extra danger. This also adds some custom advancements, enchantments, and other features that may or may not function properly. (Sleep Vote overrides usually unskippable horde nights).
  • Villager Transport allows you to use camels and llamas to transport villagers easily in a vanilla friendly way. (Untested)
  • New Battlepass is a quest giving addition for distributing Fehu, and other items with a premium version that I am still unsure of how to implement. Monthly resets, and updates to come.

Lands (claims + nations + war)

Lands is a land-claim / grief-prevention / nation-war plugin with taxes and upkeep. Don't get comfortable: enemy nations can declare war and conquer claims even while you're offline.

  • Pay taxes/upkeep to maintain claims.
  • Land claims cost about ᚠ1010 per chunk, and upkeep is collected at 11PM each day
  • Declare war and conquer land.
  • Rent or sell parts of your nation.
  • Control settings like fire tick and mob spawning inside claims.
